ABOUT
VIRALROOT

"Every meme has a story. We find it."

ViralRoot is a meme encyclopedia and origin database. When something goes viral — a sound, a phrase, a video, a trend — we research where it started, who created it, why it exploded, and what it's called.

Our mission is simple: be the first result when someone Googles a meme. When you see something trending on TikTok or Instagram and think "where does this come from?" — we want to be your answer.

We cover trends from every platform: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube, Twitter/X, Reddit, Facebook — and from every country and culture. Memes don't respect borders and neither do we.
